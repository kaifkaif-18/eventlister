About

Anjunadeep is back in India with a plethora of shows lined up in February 2024 for the fans. After a successful open air show in Goa in December 2023 these upcoming events are going to be a musical treat and vibe with those happy smiling faces. Anjunadeep is an independent record label based in London. Founded in 2005 by Above & Beyond, and their manager James Grant, Anjunadeep began life as an outlet for records from the deeper, more progressive end of Above & Beyond’s club sets. Today, the label is one of dance music’s most respected imprints. At 17 years old, Anjunadeep has evolved a great deal since its inception, and while the BPMs may have slowed, and the label family exponentially expanded, Anjunadeep remains just as committed to finding and supporting the very best in soulful electronic music.
